E4 or D4?

The most important thing is CONSISTENCY. Pick one of these two opening moves and stick with it. The choice doesn't really matter as both opening moves provide a wide variety of options for both sides. Similarly, pick an opening as an answer for each of These pawn moves and stick with it, after you've explored some of the options. Theory isn't too important at a beginner level.
